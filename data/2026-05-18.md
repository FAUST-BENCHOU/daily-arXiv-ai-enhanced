Method: 理论分析（抽象上下文内容，仅依赖序列长度证明RoPE注意力变得不可预测）+ 实证分析（验证多头多层无法克服）。

First, it loses its locality bias: RoPE is no more likely to favor nearer positions than substantially farther ones.
